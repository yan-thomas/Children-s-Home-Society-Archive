Historical Timeline

Explore the history of the Children's Home Society through key milestones and archival records.

Key Milestones

  • 1883Early orphan care records — first intake documents and handwritten ledgers.
  • 1899State incorporation; first official charter signed by founding board.
  • 1910New facility photographs document expanded campus buildings.
  • 1927First newspaper feature in the State Gazette highlights adoption services.
  • 1942Wartime correspondence; care records during increased demand for services.
  • 1958Annual report shows landmark milestone of 1,000 placements.
  • 1965Federal policy shift — documents reflect new child welfare legislation.
  • 1981Photographic survey of all facilities and programs commissioned.
  • 2004Digitization project begins; archive formally catalogued.
